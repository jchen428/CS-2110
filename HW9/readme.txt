--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2 Fat 2 Furious
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Advance through four levels of increasing difficulty as Fat Guy from Super Mario
World 2: Yoshi's Island by devouring all you can while avoiding contact with the
deadly broccoli.

Controls
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
D-Pad.......Move
Start.......Continue to next level when prompted
Select......Reset game
A...........Advance to next level (for debugging)
B...........Advance to loss-state (for debugging)
